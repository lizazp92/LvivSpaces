About us

LvivSpaces is a full-scale innovative coworking space in Lviv, created by a creative team of professionals. Our team is represented by talented creators with non-standard thinking, who strive to turn your workspace into a cozy and comfortable environment.

LvivSpaces offers you high-class working conditions that meet all global trends in creating a high-quality business environment. Our co-working space will change your perception of what renting an office in Lviv is like.

Choose the best option for your needs:

Day Pass

For those who need a flexible and comfortable workspace for one day without long-term contracts.

Open-space

For those who prefer to work in an open space, in a circle of like-minded people and colleagues. Your fixed workplace 24/7.

Private Office

For those who want their own space. Private offices-transformers for maximum comfort and concentration on teamwork.

Meeting rooms

For fans of endless negotiations: meeting rooms are equipped with everything necessary for negotiations and meetings: screen, chromecast, webCamera, flipcharts/marker walls, pens/pencils/markers, stickers.
